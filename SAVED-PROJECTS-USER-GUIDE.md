# Saved Projects - User Guide

## What are Saved Projects?

Saved Projects is like a bookmark feature that lets you save papers you're interested in for easy access later. Think of it as your personal collection of favorite capstone papers!

---

## How to Save a Paper

### Step 1: Browse Papers
Go to the **Projects** page (Student Dashboard)

### Step 2: Find a Paper
Use filters or scroll to find a paper you like

### Step 3: View Details
Click the **"View"** button on the paper card

### Step 4: Save It
In the popup modal, click **"Add to Saved Projects"**

### Step 5: Confirmation
You'll see a green notification: ‚úÖ "Paper added to Saved Projects"

**That's it!** The paper is now saved to your collection.

---

## How to View Your Saved Papers

### Navigate to Saved Projects
Click **"Saved Projects"** in the top navigation menu

### What You'll See
- All papers you've saved
- Same filtering options as main dashboard
- Count of saved papers
- "Remove from Saved" button on each paper

---

## How to Remove a Saved Paper

### Step 1: Go to Saved Projects
Click **"Saved Projects"** in the navigation

### Step 2: Find the Paper
Scroll or use filters to find the paper

### Step 3: Remove It
Click **"Remove from Saved"** button on the paper card

### Step 4: Confirmation
You'll see a notification: ‚úÖ "Removed from saved projects"

The paper disappears from your saved collection immediately.

## Filtering Your Saved Papers

You can filter your saved papers just like the main dashboard:

### Search Box
Type keywords to search in:
- Paper titles
- Author names
- Field names

### Field Filter
Select a specific field:
- All Fields
- IoT
- Database

### Year Range
Filter by publication year:
- From Year: Minimum year
- To Year: Maximum year

### Reset Filter
Click **"Reset Filter"** to clear all filters and see all saved papers

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I save the same paper twice?
**A:** No, if you try to save a paper that's already saved, you'll see a message: "This paper is already in your Saved Projects"

### Q: How many papers can I save?
**A:** There's no limit! Save as many as you need.

### Q: Can other students see my saved papers?
**A:** No, your saved papers are private and only visible to you.

### Q: What happens if I remove a paper by mistake?
**A:** You can always save it again by going back to the Projects page and clicking "Add to Saved Projects" again.

### Q: Do saved papers expire?
**A:** No, they stay saved until you manually remove them.

### Q: Can I save papers from the Guest view?
**A:** No, you need to be logged in as a student to save papers.
